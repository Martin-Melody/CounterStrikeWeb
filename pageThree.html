What is Counter-Strike

Brief History of CounterStrike
Counter-Strike (also known as Half-Life: Counter-Strike or Counter-Strike 1.6)[4] is a first-person shooter video game developed by Valve. It was initially developed and released as a Half-Life modification by Minh "Gooseman" Le and Jess Cliffe in 1999, before Le and Cliffe were hired and the game's intellectual property acquired.[More info]
